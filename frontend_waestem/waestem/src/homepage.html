France

France is a country located in Western Europe, known for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum. It's famous for its world-renowned cuisine, wine, and fashion, as well as its rich history and culture.

Italy

Italy, located in southern Europe, is renowned for its rich history, culture, and cuisine. It boasts some of the world's most famous landmarks, including the Colosseum and the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and is home to numerous iconic cities such as Rome, Florence, and Venice.

Germany

Germany, located in Central Europe, is known for its historic landmarks, world-class museums, beer, and engineering expertise. It's a country that has played a pivotal role in shaping modern European history.
